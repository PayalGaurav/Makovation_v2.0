Event Segments

Two Phases - Ideation , Working

Ideation Phase

Here, the students will ideate and present it in descriptive form according to the theme selected either in group or individually

Working Phase

Here, the students either in group or individually would be presenting a working model at the end of the event on which they will be marked and judged upon.

Theme Description

Five Themes

Theme 1
Environment

We are on borrowed time when it comes to environment. Rapid climate changes have given rise to innumerable problems. Students are encouraged to ideate or build a product that help humanity to use our resources in a sustainable manner. We have one home- earth.

Theme 2
Technical Business

Who doesn't want to create the next tech giant, but can you do it in next two days? Give it a shot and see if you can be the next Steve Jobs! You ideate, we'll take care of the rest.

Theme 3
Non-Technical Business

Offline businesses need to up their game in the midst of the plethora of online businesses. If things are the way they are, offline businesses will be a thing of the past. Can you come up with an offline business model/idea that can compete with the Amazons of today?

Theme 4
Social Ventures

We'll prove them wrong who think entrepreneurship is a selfish venture. This section of Makovation is focused on bringing positive change at societal level by creating job oppurtunities and security for the underprivileged.

Theme 5
Unconventional Innovations

If fidget spinners can take over the market in days, so can anything. If you have a crazy idea that you believe can generate revenue, come ideate with us.

Frequently Asked Questions

...

What are the benefits of Makovation? Why should I participate?

Makovation provides the best platform for innovations to get the right mentorship and recognition.

We encourage budding innovators and entrepreneurs to work on an idea/ innovate and hence promote the startup culture. Your idea can be the next million dollar company.

How can I participate?

Students can participate either individually or in groups. You need to register through the registration link and select your respective theme and phase.

Are there any fees for this event?

No. There is no fees for participating in Makovation. You just need to register and take part to show your idea or working model and get a chance to win amazing prizes.

Your idea will get a boost from our experienced mentors and judges..
